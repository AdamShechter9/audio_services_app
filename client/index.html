Affordable

As fellow musicians, we know what it's like to be on a budget, and how hard it is to survive in the industry. We do our best to make our services affordable, and will offer bulk rate for Albums and labels.

We guarantee our sound will be unmatched over loudspeakers or headphones!

The Process

Our engineer, Adam, carefully listens to the submitted music for any audible errors or issues with the submission. If everything sounds and looks ok, we come up with references from similar genres.
Next, a spectrum analyzer and level meters are used to study your song and look at the frequencies and dynamics of the various instruments.
Now the mastering engineer carefully adjusts your song through plug ins, including but not limited to: Equalizers, Stereo Wideners, Mutliband Compressors, and finally Limiters.
Finally, our engineer takes another critical listen and see if anything needs a readjustment, before it is being uploaded and a link is sent.
