Adam Shechter

Berklee College of Music alumnus (2010)


Adam has been writing electronic music and studying electronic production for over 10 years...
Adam honed his audio engineering and sound design skills while attending Berklee College of Music, working as a computer recording tutor for the school. After graduation, destiny brought Adam to Miami where he worked at the world-famous South Beach Studios under the legendary Tom Lord Algae with acts like Pharrell Williams, Cee-Lo Green, Gloria Estefan and Lights Over Paris.
As of 2015, Adam relocated to sunny California. As Warp9, Adam has released albums and singles that reached #1 spot in Glitch hop categories on Beatport and Juno.
Adam shares his time developing Web and Mobile Applications and audio engineering.
