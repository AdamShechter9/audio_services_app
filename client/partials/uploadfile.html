Guidelines
  • Please name your song as: artist - song (radio mix)
  • Compress your songs to a single zip file
  • Minimum is 16Bit 44.1Khz Stereo WAV (higher rates is fine)
  • Leave 3dB to 6dB of headroom in the mix
  • Compressors/Limiters on the master bus should be deactivated
  • You should be as happy as possible with your current mix
